# CarEval — PRODUCT.md

## Product Purpose
Lead-gen site for CarEval (SC VAST Expertise SRL, careval.ro) — automotive evaluations and judicial technical expertise for accident cases. Users get an official expert report that lets them fight low insurance payouts. 100% online, delivered in 24–48h.

## Register
brand

## Users
Romanian car owners who had an accident and received a low insurance offer, people buying/selling cars, fleet managers at companies. They are stressed, feel cheated by insurers, and need a trustworthy official document fast. They are not tech-savvy. Mobile-first.

## Brand
- **Tone:** Confident, direct, on the user's side. Anti-bureaucratic. Never corporate. "We fight for your money."
- **Colors:** Deep navy `#07101f` (hero bg), lime green `#8fd02f` (accent/CTA), white text.
- **Fonts:** Space Grotesk (headings, bold editorial), DM Sans (body).
- **Personality:** Premium but accessible. Think legal expert who speaks plain Romanian.

## Anti-references
- Insurance company sites (cold, corporate, user-hostile)
- Generic car service sites (boring, outdated)
- Overloaded wizard forms with 40 fields on one screen
- Stock photo heavy designs

## Strategic principles
1. Every section answers "what do I get?" before "how does it work?"
2. Price anchoring: always show what the insurer offers vs. what CarEval recovers (+4.200 lei framing).
3. Trust signals: expert judicial authorization, AUDATEX/DAT systems, Targetare.ro awards 2023/2024.
4. One primary CTA per section max: "Cere evaluarea acum".
5. 3D floating cutout images (no background) for premium feel — established visual language.
